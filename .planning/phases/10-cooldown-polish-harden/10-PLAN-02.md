**Trigger:** When Streamlit chart quality is insufficient for production use.

**Goal:** Replace Streamlit with a NextJS + lightweight-charts frontend for TradingView-grade charts.

**What to build:**
- `frontend/` NextJS app (TypeScript)
- `lightweight-charts` (TradingView OSS library) for price + volume panels
- Multi-timeframe selector (1D, 5D, 1M, 3M, 6M, YTD, 1Y)
- Crosshair, drawing tools, indicator overlays (RSI, MACD panels)
- Real-time WebSocket feed from FastAPI (`/ws/ohlcv/{ticker}`)
- Agent signal overlays on chart (BUY/SELL markers)

**Why deferred:** Streamlit MVP sufficient for v1.0 validation. NextJS adds significant frontend complexity — justified only after user feedback confirms chart quality is a blocker.
